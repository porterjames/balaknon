Sa Ikaw Pay Bulak

by Marciano Penaranda


Di kahibudngan nga kitay magsabut

Gahapon pa ug karon ug sa umalabut

Sa kidlap sa bitoon

Ikaw ang akong duawon sa kanunay;

Kanunay ko tagoon

Kaamyon nimo igaloon,

Ug sa lubus kahidlaw ko gitiunay

Ang bulung sa nagtahan kon kalibug

Sa ikaw pay bulak ug ako pay kaibug.


Oh, sa buwan gilukut sugilanon

Gilakdan sa mga adlwang bulawanon;

Unya kining tinoohan

Mituybo sa katam-is sa imong puangud,

Putung kapangakohan,

Butah sa kabag-ohan,

Kay nagatipig sa maanindut sangud,

Kadtong panumpa nga wala ko isibog

Bisag di na ikaw bulak, wa nay kaibug!


Karon nga nia dinhi nagaatbangay

Sa tutok lolot pa kita naglangay

Mahimo atong bakwion

Kadtong sa adlaw ug buwan gilakaran;

Mahimo atong tition

Dinhi ning kagabhion

Unsay ania pang salin palaran

Sa bulung sa nagtahan kon kalibug

Sa ikaw pay bulak ug ako pay kaibug!


When You Were Still the Flower

trans. Vicente Bandillo


It is no surprise that you and I agree

Yesterday, today, and tomorrow

By the sparkle of a star

I would visit you always;

Always I would hide

Your fragrance that I sleep with,

And in my pure yearning is kept

The balm for my deepening distraction

When you were still the flower and I desire.


Oh, a story is enfolded in the moon

Caught asleep by golden suns;

And this faith grows

From the sweetness of your mercy,

Unswerving duty

Blind to changes

Because it keeps a pleasant code:

That vow I did not take back

Though you were no longer the flower, no more desire!


Now that here we sit, delaying,

Gently looking into each other's eyes,

We can claim again

What sun and moon have passed over;

We can drink to the last drop

Here in the night

What in good fortune is left

Of the balm for my deepening distraction

When you were still the flower and I desire!
